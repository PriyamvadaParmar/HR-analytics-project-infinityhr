## Assessment Reflection

### Business Problem
The organisation lacked clear, actionable visibility into workforce trends,
making it difficult to proactively manage attrition, performance risks,
and workforce stability.

### Approach Taken
The project focused on defining clear business questions, identifying
relevant HR KPIs, and translating data into insights aligned to leadership
needs.

### BA Tools & Techniques Applied
- Requirements analysis
- KPI definition and validation
- Data analysis and trend identification
- Insight generation and recommendation design

### Outcomes & Value
The analysis enabled leadership to identify high-risk workforce segments,
prioritise interventions, and adopt a more data-driven approach to HR
decision-making.

### Learning Reflection
This project reinforced the importance of stakeholder-focused analysis,
clear documentation, and communicating insights in a structured,
professional manner suitable for executive discussion.
