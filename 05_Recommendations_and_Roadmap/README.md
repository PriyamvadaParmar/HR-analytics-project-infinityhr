## Recommendations & Roadmap

### Key Recommendations
- Introduce targeted retention initiatives for high-risk departments
- Strengthen onboarding and early-tenure engagement programmes
- Equip managers with performance coaching and feedback frameworks
- Use workforce data to support proactive HR interventions

### Implementation Roadmap

#### 0–30 Days
- Validate data sources and KPI definitions
- Share dashboards with HR leadership
- Identify priority risk areas

#### 30–90 Days
- Pilot retention and engagement initiatives
- Monitor KPI movement and early outcomes
- Gather feedback from managers and HR partners

#### 90–180 Days
- Refine interventions based on results
- Scale successful initiatives across teams
- Embed dashboards into regular HR reviews
